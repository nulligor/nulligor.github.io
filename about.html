Since writing about yourself is pretty hard and I was told to start with a punchline of some sort, here you go. This is the very citation that could be said to be my all time favorite.

I can't really say if it tells a lot about me though (lol).

I'm 23, live in São Paulo - BR, been programming since I was 17, focus on web technologies and back-end engineering.
When I'm not eagerly digging deep into studying new tech I usually read sci-fi/philosophy books, watch shows from the land of the rising sun, discuss and think about the social aspects of coding or listen to some weird music nobody actually cares about.
I am currently employed as Mobile Sofware Developer at Command Alkon.
I am currently employed as Back-end Engineer at Pet Love.
I also used to attend to Math University (UNESP - BR) and Business/IT at (FATEC - BR) but now I'm eager to re-think my possibilities in any specialized STEM curriculum.
As for fun I read books and ride bikes (now more occasionaly than ever...). Sometimes I take risks and try out some new game here and there (even though nowadays I tend to be particularly grumpy towards new games).

Strangely enough, all of my interest regarding code started off back in 2007 when I had to softmod some Nintendo DS games that I owned in order to play them in english.

And I haven't stopped thinking about code ever since...
